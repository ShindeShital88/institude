PARIKRAMA COLLEGE INFORMATION


Parikrama is equipped with advanced amentities so students can explore other activities.This promotes healthy lifestyle among our students, giving them a all-round development.Giving them a significant edge over the healthy lifstyle we adapt here at Parikrama.
HOSTEL & MESS

The Trust has started a college hostel on the campus, which caters the needs of almost 400 students; the capacity will be increased still further. The inmates of the hostel get morning and evening tea, breakfast, lunch and dinner in the college mess at a reasonably cheaper rate. The feast! non vegetarian food is served twice a week. There are facilities like washing machines, electric irons, a common T. V.

LIBRARY

There is a state of the art library facility available to the students, where a large number of books, national and international journals are available to the students. All the Institutions have their own libraries and in addition to that, there is a central library and a reading room. Attractions Central library and a reading room More than 20,000 books and 100 journals Open 24x7 Image.

COMPUTER CENTER

In addition to computer laboratories, Computer Centre (CC) caters to the computational and IT related needs of the academic. The Computer Centre is equipped with the latest computer systems to encourage the students to make full use of the facility to train themselves in modern computer applications and software applications development.

SPORTS

Students are encouraged to participate and engage in the intercollegiate and inter engineering diploma students, competitions in various sport such as cricket, volleyball, weight lifting, khokho, kabaddi etc. at the zonal levels. We also encourage students to live a active life by engaging in sports. Other indoor and outdoor sports facilities are available on the campus.Attractions Large Cricket and Football Field Indoor sports facilities Encouragement for Sports Development Horse Riding.

TRANSPORT

Parikrama maintains an excellent fleet of comfortable, well maintained buses with trained staff. The transport is provided to students on first-cum-first serve basis, subject to avoilability of seats, The main aim is to provide safe, comfortable and efficient service to our students and staff.Teachers accompanying students in buses.Nearest Airport- Logon Pune(90kms) Nearest Railway Station- Daund Junction(10Kms) Fleet of 21 Buses covering distance upto 75Kms from Parikrama.

FESTIVALS

We encourage celebration of all festivals. We also host a cultural festival in college named Udaan where we receive renowned stars and influential personalities accross three days with events including dramas, music, dance and other prestigious events. We also celebrate National Holidays like Independance Day and Republic Day to develop our responsibilitiy to our nation. Udaan Cultural Festival National Festivals Encouragement to celebrate other events.
